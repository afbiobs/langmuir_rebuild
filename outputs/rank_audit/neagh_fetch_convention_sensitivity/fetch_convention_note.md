Fetch convention note for the Neagh point-summary rerun:

- The fetch bins in `point_summary_enriched.csv` are defined relative to the matched point.
- `fetch_0` is the over-water distance from the point toward geographic north.
- Therefore `fetch_0` is the relevant bin for a northerly wind, `fetch_180` for a southerly wind, and so on.
- For wave-growth fetch, the physically relevant convention is the meteorological wind `from` direction, not the downwind `to` direction.
